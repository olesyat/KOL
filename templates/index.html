About

The goal of this web-application is to visually represent the connections between the coronavirus Researches. The visualization is useful for analysing the research groups. In future, more group-specific information can be added. For example: topics of researches, keywords from publications. The thickness of connection on the graph represents number of articles written together. You can filter the graphs of co-authors by country and institution.
